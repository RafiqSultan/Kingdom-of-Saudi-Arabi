KSA(Kingdom of Saudi Arabi)

Where is Saudi Arabia located? The Kingdom of Saudi Arabia is located in southwest Asia, bordered to the west by the Red Sea,
to the east by the Arabian Gulf, to the north by Iraq and Jordan, and to the south by Yemen.

The capital of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia is Riyadh, which is the largest city in Saudi Arabia.

Traveling to Saudi Arabia does not require an entry visa and even travel for citizens of all the countries of the Gulf Cooperation Council. As for the rest of the countries, they need to obtain a Saudi visa.

The official currency in Saudi Arabia is the Saudi riyal.
